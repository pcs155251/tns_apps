\subsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename T$>$\\*
class en\+Pess$<$ T $>$}

A derived class of \hyperlink{classctmBase}{ctm\+Base} describing the environment of 3-\/\+P\+E\+SS on kagome lattice. 

The 3-\/\+P\+E\+SS ansatz with translational invariance is contracted to a 2D infinite T\+NS with single tensor unit cell. For parctical convenience I assume a 2 by 2 unit cell with 4 same tensors (say tensor A). All the measurement is performed within A tensor on the upper left corner (this should not matter if the environment is fully converged). \hyperlink{classenPess}{en\+Pess} is uesd in pess to measure the physical quantites. Methods defined in \hyperlink{classctmBase}{ctm\+Base} are employee to find the converged environment.
